Recording on Acapella

Music (10-17-2016)

Over the past five years, Youtube has helped start the careers of many musicians, including some who use their editing chops to record multiple takes and play them over each other. The concept of a one man band is hardly a new one, but the digital age has taken it two steps up- from professional productions and recording studios, and into the hands of amateur musicians like me. Now, it's possible for me to record audio and video with an iPhone and a mic, and make videos faster than the pros used to do it, and with even higher resolution than ever.
