Eric

Gourmand

There's really no better description for this. Ever since my parents brought chicks (baby chickens) home in 10th grade, I've been borderline obsessed with sourcing my food as best I can. We treat our animals well, and feed them a nutritious diet with plenty of space to free range and scratch dirt- the results are drastically improved eggs, even when compared to store bought organic.
Since then, my interests have slowly shifted to include gardening, cooking, food science, and physical fitness, because there's no better feeling than eating tasty food and feeling great about it.

About Me

Eric Zhang - Undergraduate, Tech Enthusiast, Hacker

I am a junior bioengineering student at the University of Pittsburgh minoring in chemistry and mechanical engineering. I'm always curious to see how new technologies effect the way we interact with the world, and love to share that knowledge with others. I got started with that in sophomore year as the workshop coordinator for Design Hub, a student organization centered around the education and application of useful skills to real world problems.

I was accepted into the Microsoft Student Partner program just before junior year began, which provided me with both training and resources to teach myself and others new and useful skills. This website is one noteworthy example of the results (hosted by Azure, created on 10/08/2016).

ericsibored

Pianist, Cellist & Musician

As a classically trained pianist, I spent 8 years playing solo classical repertoire before picking up the cello and playing with orchestras, chamber groups, and garage bands for another 7 years before college. Groups like ThePianoGuys and 2Cellos have had a major influence on my style and arrangements, as well as my appreciation many genres pop and classical to metal and rock.
Now that I have less time to collaborate with other musicians in person, I spend my time recording myself with others on the Acapella app @ericsibored .
